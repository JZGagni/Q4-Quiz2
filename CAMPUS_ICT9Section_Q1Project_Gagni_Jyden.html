Juan Christostomo Ibarra
  • Ibarra is a wealthy, youthful man that has recently came back to the Phillipines after studying in Europe.
  • He is very selfless, smart, and well respected in the San Diego Community.
    		•
    Maria Clara
  • Maria Clara is one of the main characters in Noli me Tangere.
  • She is also the daughter of Kapitan Tiago and is in one of the most highest social positions in San Diego
    		•
    Padre Damaso
  • Padre Damaso is the former Head priest of one of the religious factions in San Diego. He is also a very corrupt, elderly, and endlessly sly person in the story.
  • He is very prideful, thinking that all filipinos are below him and that he has more power than a king.
    		•
    Padre Salvi
  • Padre Salvi is the successor of Padre Damaso to the seat of the head priest.
  • He was also the town curate and had a vicious rivalry with the town's alfarez.
    		•
    Kapitan Tiago
  • Kapitan Tiago is the most wealthy man in San Diego, making money from his land.
  • He is also the father of Maria Clara and is one of the most trusted friends of the friars.
